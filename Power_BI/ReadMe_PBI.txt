# 📊 AdventureWorks Sales Dashboard

This interactive Power BI dashboard presents a comprehensive overview of AdventureWorks sales performance, reseller activity, and product trends. Built from a cleaned and structured dataset, it is designed to help stakeholders understand key sales drivers and identify areas for business growth.

---

## 🧩 Data Sources

The dashboard is powered by the AdventureWorks Sales dataset, which includes the following tables:

- **Sales_data**
- **Customer_data**
- **Reseller_data**
- **Product_data**
- **Date_data**
- **Sales Order_data**
- **Sales Territory_data**

Data was preprocessed using Python to clean placeholders, fix relationships, and convert date formats before being imported into Power BI.

---

## 📈 Page 1 – Sales Performance Overview

**Key Insights:**
- 💰 **Total Sales**: Displays total revenue (e.g., $42.9M)
- 📈 **Year-over-Year Growth**: Shows percentage growth in sales
- 🧱 **Order Quantity by Category**: Bar chart comparing product categories
- 🍰 **Sales Breakdown by Category**: Pie chart highlighting revenue share by category
- 📉 **Sales Trend Over Time**: Line chart tracking monthly performance

---

## 🌍 Page 2 – Reseller & Product Breakdown

**Key Insights:**
- 🏆 **Top 5 Resellers by Sales & AOV**: Highlights the best-performing resellers and their average order value
- 🌐 **Geographic Sales Distribution**: Map showing reseller activity by location
- 📊 **Sales Volume Trend by Category**: Area chart illustrating category-level sales volume across months
